## The Project
- **Client**: A Global Capital Investment Firm (confidential)
- **Name**: TDD and EDD for Hanjin Shipyard - Subic Bay
- **Theme**: TDD, EDD

### Objectives

* Phase 1
  - Visual Inspection of facilities of the Shipyard;
  - Review historical records on corrective and interventive Intervention;
  - Risk ranking for facilities;
  - Estimation of high level CAPEX based on risk scores and areas.

* Phase 2
  - Assessment and evaluation of condition and performance of assets
  - Create layout drawings of ultilities for tenant's seperation purposes. This will be used as inputs for layers to draft contracts with prospective tenants
  - Cost study for 50 years for all facilities and ultilities
  - Create a master schedule for intervention works for the next 2 years
